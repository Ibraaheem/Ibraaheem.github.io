About Us

Properties 20/20 is a group of property developers established in Cape Town. Our core focus is the provision of affordable homes offering a range of housing solutions to our clients. Properties 20/20 believes in providing an exceptional and comprehensive service to clients with commitment from all partners, by drawing on our extensive expertise in the construction industry.

At Properties 20/20, we aim to maintain a high level of integrity and professionalism for our clients. By utilizing a personalized approach, we are able to cater to your needs as a homeowner and deliver quality homes at affordable rates.
